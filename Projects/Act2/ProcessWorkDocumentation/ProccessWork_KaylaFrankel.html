Process Work for Act 2: Interactive Elements

Phase 1: Brainstorming

For this first phase, I started researching and brainstorming. With the subject of CURSOR, I began thinking about what it actually means to BE a cursor. Is it the fact that it points to things on a page so the user knows where they are; or is it the ability to click / select something on a page? After having many thoughts over the span of many days, a brilliant concept leapt into my head: To do all of it.

In order to showcase the different funcations and abilities of the Cursor, I create a search and click abstract site. The instructions for the site are in blue: "Glide and Search then Click and Enlarge."

GLIDE AND SEARCH: One will hover over different sections of the page to see the cursor differently. They'll see its different functions such as 'HELP' (a question mark), 'WAIT' (a blue spinning circle), 'NOT-ALLOWED' (a crossed-out mark), 'ZOOM-IN' (a magnifying glass), and 'GRAB' (a hand). The secret function: if someone were to try and highlight the page, they'd see hidden messages that help the funcations to work.
