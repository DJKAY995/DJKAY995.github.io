Exercise 1 : Self-Portrait

Hey hey everyone! My name is Kayla Frankel and I’m going into my 4th year of advertising.

This website takes prompts that you’ve written and, based on the style you pick and what you wrote, AI will create a piece of art work for you.

The prompt I wrote, which is listed below the AI’s art work, is a list of what my overall striking features are: My blonde curly hair, my red lipstick that I always put on before leaving the house (even under the mask) , my blue eyes, and a smile that I frequently wear.

This was the result…

I tried a few different styles and ended up going with a Van Gogh aesthetic, not just because it was one of the better looking portraits but also because I find his overall work to be striking and on the more interesting side of art due to its textural look. The shape of the face is also a staple of how Van Gogh would paint his own portraits.

What I love about this self-portrait and what speaks to me the most about it, is that it isn’t perfect - just like me. There are features that are more exaggerated (the eyes, the eyebrows, the lips) which definitely relates back to my style of art: more abstract and ‘weird’.
